# Business Value

`scMechanism Agent` targets a common gap between clinical questions and reproducible single-cell analysis.

Potential value:

- reduce time spent diagnosing public dataset formats.
- standardize analysis planning and safety wording across projects.
- help non-programming researchers generate auditable analysis scripts.
- improve result review by requiring marker, enrichment, communication, pseudotime, and limitation checks.
- support training, research service delivery, and translational project triage.

The near-term product form is a platform Skill that guides researchers and produces scripts/reports. A later product form could connect the same Skill to approved MCP tools, local compute services, or institutional workflow servers for more automated execution.
