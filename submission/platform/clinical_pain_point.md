# Clinical Pain Point

Clinical and translational researchers often find public single-cell datasets but struggle with:

- identifying whether GEO files are 10x matrices, h5ad/RDS objects, FASTQ/SRA files, or result tables.
- deciding whether a dataset can be analyzed directly or first needs raw-read reconstruction.
- selecting a defensible analysis route for QC, annotation, marker genes, enrichment, CellChat, and pseudotime.
- converting course or tutorial scripts into reproducible local scripts.
- interpreting computational results without overstating mechanism or clinical relevance.

This skill turns those recurring decisions into a structured, reusable workflow.
